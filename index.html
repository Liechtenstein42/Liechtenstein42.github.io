About the Team

Welcome to the WMC Robotics Home Page! As the schools robotics team, it's our duty to represent by building strong, reliable, and innovative robots. We actively participate in the VEX Robotics tournament every year, and have successfully made it to the final bracket multiple times. We represent and stand for robot takeover quality enginering and design, impactful coding, and precise execution. We strive to prepare for the worst, and thrive at our best. We are actively trying to expand our program, and are more than willing to accept new members.
